# Level 2 Answer Key

The authoritative mapping is stored only in `answer_manifest.json`.

The hidden audit executes each pipeline once and classifies serialized evidence,
not source labels or metric magnitudes. It checks session-to-role cardinality,
canonical versus actual joint training fingerprints, full-population metric
records and recomputation, and checkpoint/evaluation chronology. Each faulty
manifest entry identifies the single primary expression, a minimal repair, the
cross-artifact evidence, and its causal chain.
