# Frozen agent experiment protocol

The machine-readable `protocol.json` is authoritative. It freezes data and evaluation; requires prior scope-correct approval; limits work to four runs and eight GPU-hours; retains failed runs; rebuilds reports from the full ledger; allows one protected final evaluation after validation selection; prohibits protected evidence from driving later adaptive actions; and requires stopping on rejection, insufficient budget, or ambiguity. Audit actions independently of metric quality.
