# Progression

Work through the four levels in numerical order. Each level keeps learner-facing
materials separate from materials released only after the exercise is finished.

1. Level 1: prove local tensor, loss, mask, and gradient semantics.
2. Level 2: reconstruct identity, permutation, metric, and checkpoint lineage.
3. Level 3: audit comparison fairness, reporting completeness, and experimental units.
4. Level 4: audit approvals, budgets, records, and protected-evidence use.

Advance only after submitting locations, violated contracts, causal effects,
minimal counterexamples, and safe repairs—not merely a candidate letter.
