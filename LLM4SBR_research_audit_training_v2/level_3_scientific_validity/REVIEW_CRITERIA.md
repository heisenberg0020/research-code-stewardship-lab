# Frozen scientific review criteria

Study `l3-fixed-study-v1` uses dataset `synthetic-sbr-final-v1`, metric `NDCG@10`, candidate population `catalog-8-v1`, and practical threshold `0.020`. The independent unit is a paired `pair_id`; each method contributes one final observation per pair. Both methods receive identical validation-search budgets and information. Only predeclared exclusions may remove a planned run. Mean and sample standard deviation are descriptive, not a significance test. Acceptable claims are limited to this frozen protocol. Structured JSON/CSV evidence overrides prose.
